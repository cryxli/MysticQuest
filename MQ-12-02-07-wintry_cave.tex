\subsection{Wintry Cave}
\label{map:wintry_cave}

You will eventually get caught in a landslide. \nameref{char:phoebe} will give you her \nameref{weapon:cat_claw} and start using the \nameref{weapon:bow_of_grace} instead.

After you defeat \nameref{monster:squidite}, you get the \nameref{item:libra_crest}. With it you can activate the teleport pad in the \nameref{map:libra_temple}.
