\subsection{Level Forest}
\label{map:level_forest}

The \nameref{char:old_man} hints to visit the town of \nameref{map:foresta} in search of \nameref{char:kaeli}. Around the next corner another old man asks the hero to move a boulder blocking the way to \nameref{map:foresta}. Doing so earns the hero the \nameref{item:tree_wither}. 

When visiting the forest with \nameref{char:kaeli} you face a \nameref{monster:minotaur} that will poison \nameref{char:kaeli} before you can slay him. After the fight you get her \nameref{weapon:axe} and have to retrieve the \nameref{item:cure_potion_uniq} from the \nameref{map:sand_temple}.
