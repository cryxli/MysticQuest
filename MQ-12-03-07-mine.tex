\subsection{Mine}
\label{map:mine}

The mine does not have a complex playout. What makes it difficult to navigate are the many one way tracks and counter-weight elevators.

It also contains the \nameref{weapon:charm_claw}. To get it move north and follow the path to the second side cavern.

Eventually, you want to find an beat the \nameref{monster:jinn} to exit the mine on the other side. There \nameref{char:reuben} throws a bomb to move the boulder freeing his father and opening the way to the \nameref{map:sealed_temple}.
