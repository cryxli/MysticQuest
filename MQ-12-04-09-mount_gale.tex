\subsection{Mount Gale}
\label{map:mount_gale}

\textit{Mount Gale} is quite a linear dungeon with some dead ends. Once you find \nameref{monster:dullahan} in the center and beat him, the wind in \nameref{map:windia} stops and you can talk to \nameref{char:otto}, again.

He will start the machine that creates the rainbos bridge to \nameref{map:pazuzus_tower}. The way is clear to find \nameref{char:norma}.
