\subsection{Bone Dungeon}
\label{map:bone_dungeon}

When facing the first caved in passage on B2, \nameref{char:tristam} teaches the hero how to use \nameref{weapon:bomb}s.

After you kill \nameref{monster:flamerus_rex}, \nameref{char:tristam} gives you the \nameref{item:cure_potion_uniq} and leaves. When you give the cure to \nameref{char:kaeli} back in \nameref{map:foresta}, she tells you to visit \nameref{char:spencer} in \nameref{map:aquaria}.

When you revisit the area without \nameref{char:tristam} the brown chests will no longer contain \nameref{weapon:ninja_stars} but \nameref{weapon:bomb}s.
