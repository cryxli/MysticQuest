\subsection{Giant Tree}
\label{map:giant_tree}

When \nameref{char:kaeli} talks to the \nameref{map:giant_tree}, he asks you to defeat the monsters within him. Once you defeated \nameref{monster:gidrah} all monsters disappear and you're free to loot the remaining chests. The tree will carry you to the other side of the forest and you can continue south.

All the faces on the wall are doors that open when you poke them with your sword. You will need the grappling mechanic of the \nameref{weapon:dragon_claw} extensively. Mushrooms can be cut down just like trees.
