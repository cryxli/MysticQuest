\section{Kaeli}
\label{char:kaeli}

\includegraphics[height=2cm,keepaspectratio]{./resources/chars/kaeli}

She lives in Foresta. Once you show her the \nameref{item:tree_wither} she'll join you to investigate why the plants are dying, helping you clear the way through the \nameref{map:level_forest} in the process.

\vspace{1em}

She will rejoin you later to get through the Alive Forest and the Giant Tree. And again through Pazuzu's Tower.
