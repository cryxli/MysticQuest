\section{Captain Mac}
\label{char:mac}

\includegraphics[height=2cm,keepaspectratio]{./resources/chars/mac}

Captain Mac is \nameref{char:kaeli}'s father, and sails a ship in the lake between Windia and Aquaria. When the lake dries up, he is stranded.

Once his ship is freed, you can use it to reach yet another entrance to the Focus Tower.
