\section{Captain Mac}
\label{char:mac}

\portraitimg{./resources/chars/mac}

Captain Mac is \nameref{char:kaeli}'s father, and sails a ship in the lake between \nameref{map:windia} and \nameref{map:aquaria}. When the lake dries up, he is stranded.

Once his ship is freed, you can use it to reach yet another entrance to the \nameref{map:focus_tower}.
