\subsection{Ice Pyramid}
\label{map:ice_pyramid}

The \textit{Ice Pyramid} has a special quirk that makes enemies invisible unless you have the \nameref{item:magic_mirror}. If you want it, hug the right wall from the entrance until you find the red chest containing it.

Another specialty are the dark wall statues. They act like switches when you poke them with a sword, usually opening a hidden door in a wall close to them.

To get the \nameref{armor:noble_armor} you may want to purposefully drop from the top floor. You can only reach the red chest containing it that way.

Once the \nameref{monster:ice_golem} is defeated, \nameref{char:phoebe} leaves for \nameref{map:aquaria}. Meet her there and visit \nameref{char:spencer} in the caverns under her house. Don't forget the \nameref{item:river_coin} that opens another entrance to the \nameref{map:focus_tower}.

\nameref{char:spencer} gives you the \nameref{item:venus_key} to open a chest in the \nameref{map:focus_tower} containing the \nameref{armor:venus_shield}. He also tells you about his plan to rescue his friend, \nameref{char:mac}.

Take the route north of the \nameref{map:libra_temple} to enter the \nameref{map:focus_tower} from this side. You cat get the \nameref{armor:venus_shield} and \nameref{spell:blizzard} and meet the \nameref{char:old_man} again. He tells you to look for \nameref{char:reuben} in \nameref{map:fireburg}.

When entering the \nameref{map:focus_tower} through the lower Auqaria entrance, you can open another door with the \nameref{item:river_coin}.
