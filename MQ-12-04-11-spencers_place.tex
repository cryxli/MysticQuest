\subsection{Spencer's Place}
\label{map:spencers_place}

Onto entering the place using the extended rainbow bridge, \nameref{char:reuben} throws a \nameref{weapon:mega_grenade} releasing the waterfall into the lake, filling it once again. You see \nameref{char:mac}'s ship drift towards \nameref{map:ships_dock}. 

Before you report back to \nameref{char:kaeli} in \nameref{map:windia} you may want to get the \nameref{item:mobius_crest}.

\nameref{char:kaeli} gives you the \nameref{item:captain_cap} to show to \nameref{char:mac} once you find him on \nameref{map:macs_ship}. Take the teleport pad in the northern house to reach it.
