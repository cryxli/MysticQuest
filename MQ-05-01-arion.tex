\section{Arion}
\label{char:arion}

\includegraphics[height=2cm,keepaspectratio]{./resources/chars/arion}

He is a miner from Fireburg that has been trapped by a landslide. His son \nameref{char:reuben} urges you to help him rescue his father.

He will give you the \nameref{item:thunder_rock} once you defeat \nameref{monster:pazuzu}.
